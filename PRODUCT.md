## Users

**Primary (Public Portal):**
- Indonesian citizens of Tanjung Dinang seeking government administrative services
- Users of varying digital literacy accessing via mobile or desktop
- Users who need to track their service requests

**Secondary (Admin Portal):**
- Government employees at KEMENAG Tanjung Dinang
- Roles: superadmin, admin, frontdesk, kasubbag, kepala, kasi, petugas, pegawai
- Users who manage services, process requests, and generate reports

## Product Purpose

SILATAR V2 is a digital government service portal for **Kementerian Agama Tanjung Dinang (KEMENAG-TD)**, Indonesia. The application enables citizens to:

1. Browse and request government administrative services online
2. Track the status of their service requests
3. Submit required documentation digitally
4. Access information about government units and services

For government employees, it provides:
1. Dashboard for monitoring service requests
2. User and service management tools
3. Performance reporting and tracking
4. Request processing workflows

## Brand Personality

**For Public Portal:**
- Trustworthy and official — citizens need confidence in a government platform
- Accessible and clear — users of all digital literacy levels
- Warm but professional — approachable government service
- Indonesian identity — local language, culturally appropriate

**For Admin Portal:**
- Modern and efficient — employees need to work quickly
- Enhanced accessibility — glass morphism effects with good contrast
- Glass morphism with purple/blue accents — light base, subtle gradients, modern feel
- Data-focused — clear information hierarchy for dashboards and tables

## Anti-references

**Avoid:**
- Generic government portal blue (overused .gov aesthetic)
- Dense, text-heavy layouts
- Confusing navigation structures
- Cyberpunk aesthetic (current admin panel being redesigned)
- Flat gray palettes without personality
- Tiny text and cramped information density

**Avoid for Admin (new theme):**
- Heavy glassmorphism (blur on everything)
- Dark backgrounds (light mode preferred for admin work)
- Neon/cyberpunk effects
- Glitch animations or decorative motion

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity First** — Every element earns its place. Clear hierarchy, readable text, obvious actions.
2. **Trust Through Design** — Professional, official-looking interfaces that inspire confidence in government services.
3. **Accessibility as Foundation** — WCAG AA compliance, enhanced touch targets, high contrast ratios.
4. **Modern Glass Aesthetics** — Light backgrounds with subtle glass effects, purple/blue accent palette, smooth gradients.
5. **Efficient Data Display** — Tables, cards, and dashboards optimized for quick scanning and action.
6. **Consistent Component Vocabulary** — Same button styles, form controls, and patterns across all screens.
7. **Mobile-First Public, Desktop-Optimized Admin** — Public portal prioritizes mobile; admin assumes desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG Level**: AA minimum, targeting enhanced compliance
- **Enhanced Features**:
  - Larger touch targets (minimum 44px)
  - Higher contrast ratios (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text)
  - Clear focus indicators
  - Semantic HTML structure
  - Keyboard navigation support
- **Motion**: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- **Language**: Indonesian throughout (UI, error messages, documentation)
